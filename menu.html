FRESH SAUSAGES

Using only the best in locally sourced pork, we believe our handcrafted sausages are the best you can buy. Our premier sausage is a classic example of our local heritage and cuisine, so we named it after the Little Balkan region of Kansas we call home. Our menu of fresh sausages features interpretations of an American breakfast sausage, our unique beer bratwursts, and a number of seasonally influenced specialty sausage recipes.

HERATIGE PORK CUTS

Heritage pork is a combination of flavor and texture that is quickly becoming the standard fare for those who demand the best food for their families. Good pork starts with happy animals. The farms we source from humanely raise their pigs using sustainable practices, solid genetics, and are free from unnecessary hormones and antibiotics. Treat yourself, and taste the difference.

BACON

We make bacon the old-fashioned way, taking our time to slowly cure and cherrywood smoke premium pork. Our nitrite-free Maple Black Pepper is the standard flavor, but unique flavor profiles and types are available from time-to-time or by special order. This is a delicacy we encourage you to try. As our fans love to say “This stuff is smack your mamma good.”

CHARCUTERIE

Like all of our other products, The Meat Shed makes all of our charcuterie items are handmade in-house with classic practices, recipes, and top-shelf ingredients. Mortadella, smoked pastrami, bologna, summer sausage, country pâté, and coppa di testa are a sampling of some of our fan favorites.

SPECIALTIES

The Meat Shed is more than just a boutique butcher. We have developed specialty recipes to enhance your home-cooked meals. All of our condiments, pickles, soup stocks, sauces, and sauerkrauts are handcrafted. This section of our menu is ever evolving as we strive to bring you new and interesting flavor profiles to compliment all of our choicest meat treats. We have piccalilly for your omelette, bier mustard for your bratwurst, pimento cheeses for your crackers, and whiskey pickles for your pastrami.   The Meat Shed is committed to providing you with the premium ingredients to make your next meal epic.
