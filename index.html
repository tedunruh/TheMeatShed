OUR STORY

Crafting Fine Meats

The Meat Shed prides itself on keeping alive the art of crafting fine meats. Our charcuterie is uniquely delicious because we are locally sourced and make everything by hand. This makes our food irresistible and important to the culture of Southeast Kansas and the Little Balkan tradition.

The Kitchen

Locally Sourced Products

The Meat Shed name represents premium quality and classic flavors. We exist to produce better products and ingredients, better service, and give our patrons the opportunity to talk about food while shopping our lineup of tasty meat treats. We regularly set up shop at area farmers’ markets, specialty food establishments, and serve up our fare during local events. We provide our patrons with the highest quality ingredients to help them cook healthier meals that just taste better. Try it out!

BOARD OF FARE

Responsibly Handmade

Here at The Meat Shed, we craft a variety of sausages, bacon, charcuterie, fresh Heritage breed pork cuts, pickles, condiments and other seasonal items. The high quality pork we source comes exclusively from farms that humanely raise their hogs, using sustainable practices.  You can depend on our products to help you prepare nourishing food of the highest quality and taste.
